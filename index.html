About

I'm a fourth-year physics undergraduate at Peking University (BSc. expected July 2026), interested in quantum field theory, string theory, holography, and quantum gravity.

Currently I'm working with Prof. Chi-Ming Chang at YMSC, Tsinghua on fortuitous states and operators in 3d superconformal theories. Before that I spent two years with Prof. Hua Xing Zhu at PKU computing energy-energy correlations and angular distributions in collider physics, which led to a publication in JHEP.

You can reach me at zheyan@stu.pku.edu.cn.
